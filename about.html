Issues of Psychological Therapy by Expert

Is Better Help Therapy Right for You?

Better Help suits those seeking convenient, flexible therapy options at home, though in-person therapy may be better for severe issues.

What are the Different Types of Psychotherapy?

Types include Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT), Psychodynamic Therapy, Humanistic Therapy, Family Therapy, and Group Therapy.

What is a Psychological Evaluation?

It's a comprehensive assessment by a psychologist to diagnose mental health issues and guide treatment planning.

You have Difficulty Sustaining your Attention?

It could indicate conditions like ADHD, anxiety, or depression; a psychological evaluation can help identify and address underlying causes.
